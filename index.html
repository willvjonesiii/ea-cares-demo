Clinical Qualifications

What a Licensed Veterinary
Technician can do.

A Licensed Veterinary Technician (LVT) completes a 2–4 year accredited degree program, passes the VTNE national board examination, and holds an active state license. This is the same credential held by the staff administering your pet's IV, running bloodwork, and monitoring anesthesia at a veterinary hospital.

The difference between an LVT and a groomer or unlicensed pet nurse is not just training — it's legally authorized scope of clinical practice. EA Care operates under veterinary supervision, bringing hospital-level competency to your home.

All clinical procedures require a valid VCPR and veterinarian-issued protocol. EA Care operates in full compliance with New York State veterinary practice regulations.

The Story Behind EA Care

Built in the Bronx,
for the Bronx.

After years working in veterinary clinics and emergency hospitals across New York City, Estefany kept seeing the same pattern — pets stressed out by clinic environments, owners overwhelmed by logistics, and routine medical care going undone simply because getting there was too hard.

Medication schedules slipping. Injection appointments missed. Post-surgical patients not getting the follow-up they needed. Not because the owners didn't care — because the system made it difficult.

"An LVT performing an injection at a hospital is doing the exact same clinical procedure as an LVT doing it in your living room. The only thing different is the location — and that matters more than people realize."

As a New York State Licensed Veterinary Technician, Estefany holds the same clinical credentials as the hospital staff managing your pet's IV and bloodwork. EA Care exists to bring that level of expertise to your home — removing the barriers without removing the standard of care.

The Bronx is where she's from. It's where EA Care started. And it's where the need was clearest: a borough with working families, multi-pet households, and pets that deserve more than rushed annual vet visits.

What EA Care Stands For

Three commitments.
Every single visit.

🏥

Clinical Standard, No Exceptions

Every procedure is performed at the same standard as a veterinary hospital. Estefany uses the same techniques, the same documentation protocols, and the same clinical judgment she would use in a clinic setting. Your home doesn't change the standard of care — it changes the location.

🏙️

Built for NYC Pet Owners

New York City pet ownership doesn't look like the suburbs. It means no car, no easy parking, a dog that panics at the elevator. EA Care was designed for this reality — for families in the Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, and everywhere in between who need clinical care to come to them.

📋

Transparent, Documented Care

Every visit is documented. Notes, vitals, photos, and observations are recorded and available to share with your veterinarian. You always know what happened at every appointment, and your vet always has what they need to continue your pet's care.
